My Education Backgroun


Master of Science Program in Interaction Technology and Design - Umeå University

The program combines computer science and interaction design, where we learn how to research, ideate, design, develop, and test our solutions with a focus on customer needs and creating an outstanding user experience. The program has given me a broad understanding of system development, problem-solving, and how to design to satisfy users' needs.


South Korea, Exchange semester - Chung-Ang University

I am currently spending an exchange semester in South Korea, where I combine my studies with exploring Korean food, culture, and nature. I have met many interesting people from all over the world and have learned a lot about them and their cultures, something I believe will be useful in my future work.

My Strengths


UX & UI design

One of my strongest skills and passion lie in UX design. I have a keen eye for shape, color, and content hierarchy, always keeping the user’s perspective at the forefront of my design process.

My greatest strength is my creativity—I love exploring new approaches to design, blending innovative ideas with established design principles and a strong focus on accessibility. 



Front-End development

In addition to UX design, I have a strong interest in frontend development. Most of my experience is in implementing mobile applications using React Native with JavaScript, as well as developing web applications with React. My solid CSS skills allow me to experiment with colors, shapes, and UI structure—my favorite part of front-end work!

Through my education, I’ve also gained foundational knowledge in backend development. While backend isn't my primary strength, this background gives me valuable insight into what's possible when designing for users, providing a fuller perspective that I find beneficial when working on various projects.

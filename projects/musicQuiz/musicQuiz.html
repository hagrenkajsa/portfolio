MusicQuiz

Project Overview

Client

A leading global audio-streaming platform with millions of users worldwide, offering a personalized listening experience across music and podcasts.

Background

Even with a well-established product, the company was actively looking for new ways to grow — exploring opportunities beyond their core streaming experience. Music quizzes have always been a popular social activity, and the company was curious about whether that energy could translate into something new within their app.

Project Summary

I worked as a UX designer on this project, leading the research phase and driving the design of a new interactive music quiz feature. The concept lets users create, host, and participate in personalized music quizzes — with an AI-driven feature that generates questions based on the participants' own playlists. The biggest design challenge was defining the interaction between the host and participants, making sure both experiences felt seamless and logical in parallel. Through an iterative design process and continuous user testing, we refined the concept into an interactive prototype that was well received by the company — they were especially impressed by the interaction flow and how naturally it fit into their existing product.

Project Approach

Background

The opportunity

Exploring new product territory for a global streaming platform

The company had a strong core product but was actively exploring new directions for growth. Music quizzes stood out as an opportunity — a format that's genuinely fun and social, yet largely untapped in the digital streaming space. The goal was to explore what a music quiz feature could look like within their existing app, and how AI could make the experience even more personalized.

The concept

An interactive, AI-powered music quiz built into the streaming app

The idea was to let users create, host, and join music quizzes — either with friends or other music fans. Unlike most digital quizzes that only show one question at a time, we wanted to replicate the feel of a traditional pen-and-paper quiz, where participants can see all questions and go back to edit their answers at any time.

On top of that, we integrated an AI feature that generates quiz questions based on the participants' own playlists — so the quiz feels personal and relevant to whoever's playing.

Target group

Music lovers aged 20–40 who enjoy social activities and are already active users of the platform.

Research

Understanding the user

Customer and market research to identify the opportunity

I led the research phase, combining market research with a quantitative user study targeting people with an interest in music quizzes. The survey was deliberately framed around traditional, non-digital quiz experiences — this helped us uncover genuine needs and motivations without anchoring respondents to the idea of a digital product.

The insights confirmed that music quizzes are a popular social activity with clear unmet needs in the digital space — particularly around flexibility, personalization, and the ability to engage with all questions throughout the game. These findings shaped the core design decisions going forward.

Design

Iterative design process

From early concepts to a refined interactive prototype

The design went through multiple iterations — each round of user testing surfaced new insights that pushed the concept further. Testing methods included A/B testing and Think-Aloud sessions, which gave us a clear read on what was working and what needed refinement. We also designed within the company’s existing visual guidelines, which let us focus fully on optimizing the interaction flow rather than spending time on visual decisions.

The dual-interface challenge

Designing seamless parallel interactions for host and participants

The most complex design challenge was building two interfaces that had to work in sync — one for the quiz host and one for the participants. Every action the host took had a direct effect on what participants saw, and vice versa. I focused on mapping out a clear information flow between the two and making sure the sequence of events felt logical and natural from both sides. Getting this right was key to the overall experience feeling smooth and intuitive.

Result

Create quiz

This flow lets the user build a quiz from scratch — choosing from question templates or using the AI generator to instantly create personalized questions based on the participants' playlists.

1. Create a new quiz.

2. Name the quiz.

3. Empty quiz – now you can add questions.

4. Add a question and choose between different question templates.

5. Add a song for the question.

6. Set the interval for the part you want to play for this question.

7. Based on the question template and chosen song, the answers for the question are generated. They can also be edited if needed.

8. The first question is now added.

9. To create questions with the AI generator, press the marked button.

10. Multiple questions are now generated. The songs in these questions are based on the participants’ playlists.

Host quiz

This is the host’s view during a live quiz — controlling which questions are active, navigating between them, and reviewing participants’ answers when the quiz wraps up.

1. Invite friends and start the quiz.

2. The quiz has started, but no questions are currently active. You can play the questions in any order. When a question is played, its answer form becomes available to all participants.

3. Start Question 1. Click on the question or drag the bar below to view more details.

4. View detailed information about the question. Use the number carousel below to navigate between questions. The active question is highlighted in green.

5. This is what it looks like when you navigate to a question that is not currently being played for the participants.

6. Now you are playing Question 2. This question includes four songs. You can either press the play button to play them in order, or select specific songs if you do not want to play them all.

7. When finished, you can review and correct the participants’ answers.

8. Overview of all the questions in the quiz.

9. Open to view the participants’ answers. The app automatically corrects them, but you can manually adjust the points if needed.

10. When you are done, you can present the results to the participants.

11. The winners are then displayed.

Participate in quiz

This is the participant’s experience — joining the quiz, answering questions at their own pace, and seeing the results at the end.

1. Join the quiz via QR code or link.

2. Wait for the host to start the quiz.

3. The quiz has started and Question 1 is now playing.

4. Click on the question to open the answer form. The active question is highlighted in green.

5. Type your answer. You can always go back and edit it later.

6. Move to the next question and wait for it to become active.

7. If you are unsure about an answer but want to leave a note for later, you can add it in the highlighted box.

8. If you have added a note to a question, it will be indicated by the notebook icon.

9. Once the quiz is finished, the winners will be displayed.

10. Then you can take a closer look at your result and, if needed, appeal it to the host.

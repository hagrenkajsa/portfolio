My Projects

Truck Security

#User Research | #Service Design

When concluding my studies, I completed my master’s thesis project in collaboration with Volvo Group within their Experience Design team. The focus of my research was to develop a tangible concept for a holistic security system that enhances security for truck drivers in relation to cargo and fuel theft on the road. The project followed the Design Thinking methodology in order to understand user needs and from that, design an add-on service that creates value for all stakeholders, including the driver, fleet owners and Volvo Group.

CareSyncAI

#UX | #UI | #Front-End

As part of a school project in collaboration with a company called Daresay, my team and I designed and implemented a native mobile application for elderly home care. The purpose of the application is to facilitate documentation, communication and planning of shifts between the staff and management. #UX | #Front-End | #API integration | #Scrum

My Strengths

UX & UI Design

One of my strongest skills and passions lies in UX design, where I find it fascinating to research user behavior, perception and problems and to explore how to meet their needs with solutions that are both aesthetic and functional. I strive to create experiences that go beyond expectations. I have a keen eye for shape, color, and content hierarchy, and I always keep the user’s perspective at the forefront of my design process.

My greatest strength is my creativity. I love exploring new approaches to design, blending innovative ideas with established design principles and always a strong focus on accessibility.

Front-End Development

In addition to UX design, I have a strong interest in front-end development. Most of my experience is in implementing mobile applications using React Native with JavaScript or TypeScript, as well as developing web applications with React.

Through my education, I’ve also gained foundational knowledge in back-end development. While back-end isn't my primary focus, this background gives me valuable insight into what's possible when designing for users, providing a fuller perspective that I find beneficial when working on various projects.

CareSyncAI

As part of a course project in collaboration with Daresay, my team and I designed and implemented a native application for elderly home care. The purpose of the application is to facilitate communication and planning of shifts between the staff and management. One main function in the application is an AI feature that auto-generates a summary from the latest documentation about the elderly.

Pepper Robot

In a Human-Robot Interaction course, our group of three designed and implemented the Pepper robot to serve as a sales assistant in a cafe using both movments, verbal audio and an interactive interface that enabled the customer to interact with the robot to order food. The implementation was conducted in Choregraphe, and the UI design was created in Figma. It was a challenging and fun project, as it was the first time any of us had implemented a robot. However, when we got it to work, it was exciting to see the robot perform the tasks we had programmed.

My Strengths


UX & UI design

One of my strongest skills and passions lies in UX design, where I find it fascinating to research user behavior, perception and problems and to explore how to meet their needs with solutions that are both aesthetic and functional. I strive to create experiences that go beyond expectations. I have a keen eye for shape, color, and content hierarchy, and I always keep the user’s perspective at the forefront of my design process.

My greatest strength is my creativity. I love exploring new approaches to design, blending innovative ideas with established design principles and always a strong focus on accessibility. 



Front-End development

In addition to UX design, I have a strong interest in front-end development. Most of my experience is in implementing mobile applications using React Native with JavaScript, as well as developing web applications with React. My solid CSS skills allow me to experiment with colors, shapes and UI structure, which is my favorite part of the front-end development!

Through my education, I’ve also gained foundational knowledge in back-end development. While back-end isn't my primary focus, this background gives me valuable insight into what's possible when designing for users, providing a fuller perspective that I find beneficial when working on various projects. 



Service Design

A relatively newly discovered area for me is my interest in service design. I find it really interesting and enjoyable to research a context and design a solution where not only the design itself is important, but also the value it creates for the user, the customer and the company. I understand the importance of researching the area, defining the underlying problem, designing a solution, testing it and packaging it in a way that satisfies different types of customers.
